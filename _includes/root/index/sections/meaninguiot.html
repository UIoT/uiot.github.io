Universal, again.

UIoT, means Universal Internet of Things.

What does UIoT mean?

It means being an open source project, focused on creating the very first Internet of Things middleware, platform and architecture capable of delivering software and hardware that serves as many set of devices as possible, and the maximum set of different technologies too.

Does it really work?

We have been identifying enormous sets of complex problems that resides in our everyday life and therefore, that we need to solve. We're in constant progress, going as far as possible in a daily basis. Providing intelligent systems and aims to make devices and clients real smart objects.

So, is being Universal possible?

A real Universal platform is far from existence, but we're the nearest point out here. We're advancing with math, physics, software and electronic engineering. We know it's not going to be an easy-ride, but we strongly believe that someday the IoT will be universal.
